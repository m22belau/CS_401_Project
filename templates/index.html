About Us

Aloha! We're Berylin Lau and Elora Tonaki, and we created this website to support Hawaiʻi's veterans community.

Hawaiʻi is home to a large number of veterans, many of whom face unique challenges - especially older veterans navigating a fast-changing digital world. They often deal with geographical isolation, limited access to healthcare, financial struggles, mental health issues, and difficulty finding employment or housing.

Our mission is to make it easier for veterans to find the services and support they need. This site serves as a one-stop resource hub, where veterans can learn about VA benefits, access local services, and connect with programs that improve their quality of life.

We hope this site helps make the process a little simpler, and reminds our veterans that they're not alone - and always appreciated.

Thank you for your service!

With Aloha,

Berylin Lau and Elora Tonaki
